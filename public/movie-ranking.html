The Best Movie According to Zach

My top 3 movies of all-time

Jason Bourne Trilogy

This is about an ex special agent Jason Bourne who tries to understand who tries to figure out what kind of experiment the government did to him and any other agents like him. He does this as the government agencies like FBI and CIA try to track and stop him as they do not want their projects to be publicized.

John Wick

A film about the mythical boogyman. A group of mob men kill his dog, right after his wife died and since he has nothing left to lose, goes on a revenge rampage. He kills everyone involved. This leads to more problems as he kills some son of a mafia boss who then tries to take him out. The cycle continues and Wick kills, rinses and repeats.

Forest Gump

A bullied child who grows up to join the army. Even in his adulthood, he exhibits the thought pattern of a child. Nevertheless he goes ahead to be a hero after saving his buddies in war and starting successful companies.
